Canton Uri

The beautiful place in Swiss.

Canton Uri located in Central Switzerland. The canton's territory covers the valley of the Reuss between the St. Gotthard Pass and Lake Lucerne.

Uri attarcts so many visitors with its 39 cable cars which provide access to numerous peaks, hiking and bike trails as well as ski slopes and cross-country tracks.
